# RN-10: Dynamic Shift Timings
**Category:** Core Execution (TSK-CORE)

**Description:**
The system will calculate the sign-out timer dynamically based on the current date and day of the week, following the Spanish labor regulations and company-specific policies:
- **Standard (Mon-Thu):** 8h 35min of work + 1h mandatory lunch (9h 35min total).
- **Fridays:** 6h of intensive work.
- **Summer Period (July 16th to August 31st):** 7h of intensive work daily.
- All shifts include a configurable random delay (default 10 min) to simulate human behavior.
